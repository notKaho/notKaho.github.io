About Me

Hi, my name is Andrew Leong Jun He, but you may call me Andrew.

I am a Nanyang Polytechnic student in the School of Information Technology, pursuing a diploma in Information Technology. Before coming to Nanyang Polytechnic, I studied Electronics Engineering in a School of Electronics & Info-Comm Technology at ITE.

Why did I choose Information Technology over Electronics Engineering as my course of study?

This is because I was able to pick up programming quickly and easily during my ITE studies, I noticed my programming potential. Depsite the fact that programming is not the primary focus of Electronics Engineering I found it to be fun and satisfying. As a result, I decided that programming would be my primary focus, and I enrolled at Nanyang Polytechnic's School of Information Technology to pursue a diploma in Information Technology.
